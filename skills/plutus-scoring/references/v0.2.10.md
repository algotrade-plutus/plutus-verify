# plutus-verify 0.2.10 — scoring nuances

Deltas from 0.2.9 that affect rubric application.

> Source: [CHANGELOG.md `[0.2.10]`](../../../CHANGELOG.md).

## Per-step staging closes the runtime-mount leak

0.2.10 replaces the `-v {cwd}:/srv/repo` mount with a per-step staging
copy filtered by `.dockerignore`. The "bridge step's `ok` is conditional
on host cleanliness" caveat documented for 0.2.9 is **resolved** on
0.2.10+: the only way a Tier 3 bridge step can pass is through real DB
connectivity via declared env vars.

## Skill behavior on 0.2.10

- **Reproducible bucket** — full credit for a passing Tier 3 bridge
  step on 0.2.10+. No need to inspect host state or cache mtimes.
- **Tidy bucket** — declared `step.outputs:` becomes a signal of
  maintenance care. Audit the manifest: every step that produces
  artifacts other than `.plutus/run/<step>/` should declare them.
  Missing declarations nudge Tidy down toward mid-range. Declared
  `step.inputs:` is bonus (sign that the author thought about the
  step's surface area).
- **Standardized bucket** — orthogonal; no change.

No changes from 0.2.9.
